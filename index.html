STATEMENT

I make books in order to discover my subject. The book represents a unique, sequential mode of exploration and expression of ideas through a seemingly inexaustable combination of words and images. Typography is at the heart of my practice as it plays across the landscape of the page. In the artist books I make in my studio I am continuing the exploration of the juxtaposition of current technologies with traditional printing techniques
